Join Our Growing Team

Explore exciting career opportunities at 9USRcraft. We seek skilled, passionate professionals clearly driven by excellence and innovation. Find your role, make an impact, and grow your career with us.

Location: Navi Mumbai

Job Type: Full Time

Job Description:

We are looking for an experienced and motivated Tech Lead to join our dynamic development team at 9usrcraft. As a Tech Lead, you will play a pivotal role in leading a team of developers, guiding them through the software development lifecycle, and ensuring the delivery of high-quality software solutions. You will be responsible for both technical leadership and hands-on development.

Key Resonsiblities:

  • - Lead and mentor a team of developers, providing technical guidance and support throughout the software development life cycle.
  • - Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including designers, product managers, and other developers, to deliver robust and scalable web solutions.
  • - Design and architect software solutions, ensuring they align with business requirements and industry best practices.
  • - Implement best practices in coding, testing, and debugging to ensure the delivery of high- quality software.
  • - Hands-on development using a variety of technologies, with a focus on both front-end and back-end development.
  • - Collaborate with project managers to define project scope, goals, and deliverable.
  • - EHS Audit Management
  • - Ensure adherence to coding standards, security practices, and performance optimization.

Qualification:

  • - Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent work experience)
  • - Proven experience as a Full Stack Developer or similar role, with a minimum of 5 years of experience.
  • - Strong technical leadership skills with the ability to guide and mentor a development team.
  • - Proficiency in programming languages such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React.js, Node.js, and MongoDB.
  • - Knowledge of web servers and deployment processes.
  • - Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills.
  • - Strong communication and collaboration abilities.

Year Of Experience: 5 to 8
